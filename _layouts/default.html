About

The Ooze Is Loose is an FPS (First Person Shooter), Battle Royale game and the objective is to survive till the end. The uniqueness of the game comes from the slime creature produced by shooting at other players. The creature follows players and drains their life, and fighting it only makes it more stronger. So, have fun!
